# Coco Cookies

## Ingredients
- 200 g suger
- 200 g coconut flour
- 2 eggs

## Instructions
- Mix all together
- Turn on the oven, 180 celcius
- Form in small cookie tops on a oven plate
- Bake until light brown on the sides 15-20 min.
- For extra deliciousness, warm some chokolate and smear it on the bottom of the cookies.
